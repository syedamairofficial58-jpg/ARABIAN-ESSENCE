About Our Brand

Arabian Essence brings true Arabic fragrance heritage to the world. We specialise in oud‑based luxury perfumes, crafted using traditional Middle Eastern distillation methods. Our mission is to blend ancient scent traditions with modern luxury.

Premium Craft & Heritage

Every perfume bottle undergoes a months‑long aging process to deepen the richness of the aroma. Our artisans hand‑mix the ingredients ensuring long‑lasting projection and premium sillage.

  • Hand‑blended fragrance oils
  • 3–12 months natural aging
  • Arabic traditional distillation

Rare & Exotic Ingredients

We source premium Cambodian Oud, Taif Rose, Iranian Saffron, Ambergris, Black Musk, and ancient resins. Each ingredient is selected for quality and aroma depth.

  • Pure Cambodian Oud
  • Natural Musk Blends
  • Amber & Resin Extractions
  • Luxury Floral Essences

Our Story

Established in Dubai, Arabian Essence was born from a passion to preserve the art of traditional perfumery. Over years, we evolved our blends into premium, modern signature scents adored globally. Each perfume reflects the soul of Middle Eastern luxury.

Our Origin

Our founders traveled across the Gulf and Eastern regions to study ancient perfume‑making techniques from tribal perfumers and traditional scent masters.

Our Process

We age our oils in wooden barrels, use slow heating for distillation, and develop blends with multiple aromatic layers for luxury depth and richness.

Our Promise

We guarantee long‑lasting projection, premium quality, and a fragrance experience that speaks royalty and heritage.
